OUR MISSION

To offer help and hope to our neighbors in crisis by meeting their basic needs and providing education that enables them to navigate their way out of poverty.

ABOUT MEAC

For over two decades, MEAC has been providing vital assistance, primarily in the form of food, clothing, rent and utility assistance to individuals and families who reside in the Cincinnati area. We collaborate with the City of Cincinnati, the Cincinnati–Hamilton County Community Action Agency (CAA), the Freestore Foodbank, The Children’s Home, and the Ohio State University Extension – partnerships that have greatly increased MEAC’s ability to fulfill its mission.
