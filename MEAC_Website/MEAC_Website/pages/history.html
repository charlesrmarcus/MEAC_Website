1986

Our Beginning

We began in 1986 as a collaboration between twelve community churches along with the Madisonville Community Council consolidate community resources to better serve our neighbors.

2003

Expanded Service Area

We were able to expand to serve several of our neighboring communities from the 45226, 45208, and 45209 zip codes which includes the Oakley, East End, and Hyde Park areas.

2007

Partnership

With help from the Ohio Benefit Bank we were able to expand our services to assist clients with filling out applications for public benifits including food stamps and other such programs.
